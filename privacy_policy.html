Marble Destroy Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 1. December 2023

Thank you for playing Marble Destroy! This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and disclose information when you use our game.

Information We Don't Collect

We want to assure you that Marble Destroy does not collect any personal information from its users. We do not request, access, or store any data that can be used to identify you personally.

In-App Purchases and Ads

Marble Destroy does not contain any in-app purchases or advertisements. Therefore, we do not process any payment information, and you won't be exposed to third-party advertisements while playing the game.

Analytics and Usage Data

To improve our game and enhance user experience, we may collect anonymous usage data and analytics through third-party services. However, this data is strictly non-personal and is used solely for the purpose of understanding how players interact with the game.

Children's Privacy

Marble Destroy is designed for a general audience and does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe that we may have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately, and we will take appropriate measures to delete it.

Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. It is your responsibility to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or Marble Destroy, please contact us through raising new issue at github.

Thank you for playing Marble Destroy!
